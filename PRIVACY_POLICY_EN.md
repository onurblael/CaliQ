# Privacy Policy - FoodIQ

**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

FoodIQ ("Application") is a mobile application for calorie and nutrition tracking. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

## 2. Data Collected

### 2.1 Data Provided by the User

When you use FoodIQ, we collect the following data:

- **Profile Information:** Preferred language, weight goal (lose, maintain, gain), body weight
- **Dietary Preferences:** Dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.), disliked ingredients
- **Meal History:** Meal names, calories, macronutrients (protein, carbohydrates, fat), date/time
- **Application Preferences:** Dark/light mode, subscription plan

### 2.2 Data Not Collected

FoodIQ **DOES NOT collect:**

- GPS location
- Contacts or friend lists
- Calendar
- Photos (camera only for meal scanning, not stored)
- Sensitive health information (blood pressure, glucose, etc.)
- Browsing data or browser history

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

- Providing application features (calorie tracking, nutrition analysis)
- Personalizing AI-based recipe recommendations
- Improving user experience
- Complying with legal obligations

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Encryption in Transit

- All data transmitted between the application and servers is encrypted using **HTTPS/TLS 1.2+**
- Valid SSL certificates ensure server authenticity

### 4.2 Encryption at Rest

- Sensitive data (weight, goal, restrictions) is **encrypted on the server** using **AES-256-GCM**
- Meal history is stored securely in the database

### 4.3 Authentication

- Users authenticate through **secure OAuth**
- Session tokens are stored securely (Keychain on iOS, SecureStore on Android)

## 5. Data Sharing

**FoodIQ DOES NOT share your data with third parties**, except:

- **Processing Servers:** Data is processed on FoodIQ's private servers
- **Legal Compliance:** If required by law, competent authorities may request data (with prior notice when possible)

## 6. User Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your data in a readable format

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@foodiq.app

## 7. Data Retention

- **Profile Data:** Maintained while the account is active
- **Meal History:** Retained for 2 years after the last activity
- **Deleted Data:** Permanently removed within 30 days after account deletion

## 8. Regulatory Compliance

FoodIQ complies with:

- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation) - EU
- **LGPD** (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados) - Brazil
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act) - USA

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes through the application or email.
